About RONCO

公司基本介绍

Company Profile

我们是谁

Who We Are

荣程国际供应链科技（东莞）有限公司，位于广东东莞松山湖科技产业园区，依托成熟的国内物流网络与跨境资源，服务制造企业、贸易商、跨境电商和海外品牌方。

RONCO International Supply Chain Technology (Dongguan) Co., Ltd. is located in Songshan Lake Science and Technology Industrial Park, Dongguan, Guangdong. We serve manufacturers, traders, cross-border e-commerce sellers and overseas brands through logistics and trade capabilities.

我们做什么

What We Do

  • 国际物流：海运、空运、铁运、陆运，承接整柜、拼箱、门到门及DDP/DDU服务。
  • International logistics: ocean, air, rail and road freight, covering FCL, LCL, door-to-door, DDP and DDU solutions.
  • 国际贸易：帮助海外精品进入中国，尤其关注儿童保健品、中老年营养品、天然植物提取护肤品。
  • International trade: introducing premium overseas products to China, especially children’s health products, elderly nutrition and natural plant-extract skincare.
  • 供应链服务：产品筛选、合规咨询、进口路径规划、渠道试销与长期合作谈判支持。
  • Supply chain services: product selection, compliance consulting, import route planning, pilot sales and long-term partnership support.
